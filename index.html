Frequently asked questions

Is the platform suitable for beginners?

Yes. Learning pathways start from fundamentals and adapt to student pace.

How is child safety handled?

We use moderated interactions, parental controls, and policy-based protections for minors.

Which ages does the app support?

Learning pathways are organized by level so beginners and progressing learners can both follow a suitable track.

Which devices are supported?

Khadhami Academy is available on Android and iOS using the official store links.

How much weekly time is recommended?

Families can start with short regular sessions and scale gradually based on learner pace and goals.

Can learners study offline?

Yes. Selected lessons can be downloaded for offline access in supported plans.

How do billing, renewal, and cancellation work?

Pro is billed monthly. Cancellation should be completed before renewal. Full terms are available in the Terms of Service.

Where can parents review privacy and child safety policies?

Privacy, Child Safety, Cookie Policy, Terms of Service, and Data Deletion pages are available in the legal center.
